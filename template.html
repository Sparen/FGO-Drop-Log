Logging Procedures

Whenever possible, go to the logged column and update the counts in the column. Update the date online and here as well.

Every now and then results will be consolidated, and columns will be wiped. For this reason, the following will go into play:
Every cell will have 2 values. A to-report count, and a total count in []. The to-report count is how many drops have occurred since the last time I logged online. The total count is the total amount I have ever logged. Total count should ALWAYS be updated when I log in my local database here.
